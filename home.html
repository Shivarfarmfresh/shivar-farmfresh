✦ The Founder's Story ✦

Meet Ashwini
Ghanwat

"Every family in India deserves immunity they can trust — not from a pill, but from the earth itself. I saw my own family's turmeric being pushed aside for adulterated, cheap powders. That broke my heart. That lit my fire."

Growing up in Wai-Satara surrounded by the golden turmeric fields of the Sahyadri range, Ashwini watched generations of farmers lose their livelihood to a broken supply chain riddled with middlemen and adulteration. She made a decision: bring the farm directly to every family's kitchen table.

On International Women's Day, 8 March 2026, Ashwini officially launches Shivar Farmfresh — a bold, direct-from-farm brand with a dream to reach every household in India and eventually, the world.

✦ The Road So Far ✦

Ashwini's Journey

From a turmeric field in Sahyadri to a pan-India brand. This is just the beginning.

🌱
The Beginning
Growing Up Among Turmeric Fields
Ashwini grows up in Wai-Satara, Sahyadri — one of Maharashtra's most mineral-rich agricultural belts. She watches her family grow high-curcumin turmeric but sell it at throwaway prices to middlemen who pocket the margin. The seed of an idea is planted.
Wai-Satara, Maharashtra
😤
The Realization
Adulteration Hits Home
After discovering that popular branded turmeric powders in supermarkets often contain artificial colour, starch, and sawdust — sometimes even lead chromate — Ashwini makes a personal commitment: her family will never sell anything but pure, unadulterated turmeric.
The Turning Point
💡
The Vision
COVID Changes Everything
The pandemic brings haldi milk and immunity-boosting turmeric into every household conversation. Ashwini sees the moment clearly: families everywhere are searching for trusted, clean turmeric. She begins building the Shivar Farmfresh concept — farm-direct, FSSAI certified, zero compromise.
Farm-to-Door Concept Born

8 March 2026 · Launch Day
Shivar Farmfresh is Born
On International Women's Day, Ashwini Ghanwat officially launches Shivar Farmfresh. FSSAI certified. Direct-from-farm. Four pack sizes shipped pan-India. The mission: reach every Indian family with immunity they can trust. It's not just a brand — it's a movement.
