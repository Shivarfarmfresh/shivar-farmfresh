🚀 Your website is ready! Follow the steps below to put it online for FREE in under 5 minutes.

Option A — Netlify Drop EASIEST — No Account Needed

Drag your folder → live in 60 seconds. Free forever.

1
Download the website ZIP
Click the export button at the bottom of this page to save all files.
2
Open Netlify Drop
Go to app.netlify.com/drop — no login needed to start.
3
Drag & Drop your folder
Drag the unzipped shivar_website folder onto the Netlify page. Done — you're live!
4
Share your free URL
Netlify gives you a URL like amazing-farmfresh.netlify.app. Share it with customers on WhatsApp!
5
Optional: Your own domain
Buy shivarfarmfresh.in (~₹700/yr) and connect it free in Netlify settings for a professional URL.

Option B — GitHub Pages FREE FOREVER

Best long-term option. Free permanent hosting.

1
Create free account at github.com
Sign up — it's free. No credit card required.
2
New repository → name it shivar-farmfresh
Set it to Public. Click "Create repository."
3
Upload all 4 files
Upload index.html, admin.html, products.js, assets.js via the "Upload files" button.
4
Enable GitHub Pages
Settings → Pages → Source: main branch → Save. Your site is live at yourusername.github.io/shivar-farmfresh
