✦ The Founder's Story ✦

Meet Ashwini
Ghanwat

"Every family in India deserves immunity they can trust — not from a pill, but from the earth itself. I saw my own family's turmeric being pushed aside for adulterated, cheap powders. That broke my heart. That lit my fire."

Growing up in Wai-Satara surrounded by the golden turmeric fields of the Sahyadri range, Ashwini watched generations of farmers lose their livelihood to a broken supply chain riddled with middlemen and adulteration. She made a decision: bring the farm directly to every family's kitchen table.

On International Women's Day, 8 March 2026, Ashwini officially launches Shivar Farmfresh — a bold, direct-from-farm brand with a dream to reach every household in India and eventually, the world.
